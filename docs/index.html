What is the Cryptos Constellation?

The Cryptos Constellation is a hidden network of cryptographic knowledge, where each star represents a fundamental concept in digital security and privacy. Like stars in the night sky, these concepts are interconnected, forming a map of understanding in the digital undercommons.

Why Encryption Matters

In a world of constant surveillance and data collection, encryption is our shield. It's not just about keeping secrets—it's about protecting our right to privacy, our freedom to communicate, and our ability to learn without being watched.

The digital undercommons is a space where knowledge flows freely but securely, where learning is protected by the very tools we study.
